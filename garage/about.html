Welcome to Joblings Garage

Joblings Garage is a small and friendly family run business trading in Cullercoats, Whitley Bay established in 2002. We became a Bosch Car Service Centre in 2008.

Our aim is to be your first choice for motor repairs and servicing needs. Providing you with affordable and the very best customer service possible.

At Joblings Garage we are totally committed to providing honest and accurate advice that is in the best interest of our customer. We will back this commitment with exceptional service at a competitive price.

  • We give a 100% guarantee on all work undertaken
  • We provide a 12 months Parts and Labour warranty
  • Car services to makers recommendations at a huge saving on dealer prices
  • MOT specialist, save money by having your car serviced and MOT'd at the same time
  • Most mechanical repairs undertaken for cars or vans
  • Open 5 days a week (Mon to Fri - 8.30am to 5.00pm)
  • Most makes and all ages of cars serviced
  • Fixed price quotations for all work required
  • Free inspections and advice or help over the telephone
  • Air conditioning refills and repairs
  • Bosch Diagnostic Equipment
  • Highly Trained Technicians to Bosch exacting standards
  • We can service new vehicles and maintain your dealer warranty

As a Bosch Car Service Centre we provide first class vehicle maintenance, repairs and diagnostics for all makes of vehicles. We guarantee exceptional service at an affordable price. Our technicians are trained on the latest technologies and have the most up-to-date diagnostic equipment to pinpoint any faults in your car as quickly and accurately as possible.
