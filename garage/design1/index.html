 About

Joblings Garage is a family owned business. Glen and Amanda Jobling opened the Cullercoats workshop in January 2002. Glen had worked alongside his Father previously at his Fathers garage in Gosforth, Newcastle upon Tyne. Glen was fully trained in all aspect of vehicle repairs, servicing, and as a MOT tester. During the mid-nineties Glen worked in the USA for Mitsubishi material handling equipment. When he returned to the UK from America he thought it was time to open his own garage. So he did....

Both Glen and Mandy have been involved in running their Cullercoats garage from the start and invested everything they had in diagnostic equipment , vehicle lifts and MOT testing equipment, as the business grew they were able to invest in proper training for their staff so their apprentices could go off to vehicle training centers to attain their qualifications.

After six years of building the business they realised they required professional back-up to help the business keep up to date with ever-changing vehicle technology. As Bosch is considered by many to be the pinnacle of quality in the automotive industry they applied to join the Bosch Network in January 2008. Their application was approved and they became a Bosch Service Garage later that year.

The Bosch Car Service Network displays the Trading Standards logo and as Joblings Garage has now attained the Trading Standards approved code they are monitored regularly to ensure they maintain the highest standards of workmanship and customer care.

Joblings Garage is the largest independent garage in Whitley Bay and we promise to give our customers excellent quality, service, MOT’s and vehicle repairs at an affordable price.

We can repair most vehicles and can find most OEM parts for all types of vehicle, whatever the age or make.
