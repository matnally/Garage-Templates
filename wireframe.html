 About

Joblings Of Whitley Bay Ltd was first opened in January 2002 by husband and wife team, Glen and Amanda Jobling. Fully trained in all aspect of vehicle repairs and servicing, Glen has spent most of his working life based in the motor trade.  Both Glen and Amanda have been involved in the successful running of Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd from the start, and invested heavily in electronic diagnostic equipment, vehicle lifts & MOT testing equipment together with skilled staff.

In 2002 Stefan Pietruszka joined Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd as an apprentice mechanic, and successfully completed his City and Guilds advanced automotive maintenance and repair qualification.

After six years of trading as Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd, an important company investment was made in order to ensure that the garage kept up to date with the ever-changing vehicle technology. So in January 2008 Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd applied to join the prestigious Bosch network. Bosch are considered to be the pinnacle of quality within the automotive industry. Later that same year the application was approved and Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd became a Bosch approved service garage, a status that they continue to maintain year upon year.  The Bosch car service network displays the trading standards logo, and Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd now attains the trading standards approved code. At Joblings, we pride ourselves on regular monitoring in order to maintain the very highest standards of workmanship and customer care.

After a further 12 years of experience and commitment at Joblings of Whitley Bay Ltd, Stefan took the opportunity to work in partnership with Glen and became joint company Director in 2015.

Working alongside Glen and Stefan are two very hardworking and committed motor mechanics Nigel and Michael, who together have a wealth of vehicle care and maintenance. knowledge and experience.

Joblings Of Whitley Bay Ltd is now the largest independent garage in Whitley Bay and we promise to give our customers excellent quality, service, MOT’s and vehicle repairs, at an honest and affordable price.  As an independent garage we can repair most vehicles and can find most OEM parts for all types of vehicle, whatever the age or make. Contact Joblings of Whitley Bay today and we will be more than happy to assist you in your everyday motoring needs.

Thank you,

Glen and Stefan.
